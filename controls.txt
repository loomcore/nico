TOP
===
- menubar, click on menus as normal
- question view, answer view, click
  on check button to check

ON CANVAS
=========
- double-click to init blank circle
- right-drag circle to move
- right-drag circle to bin to remove

ON CIRCLE
=========
- left-click to add placeholder
- right-click on arg to change
- ctrl-drag another circle to arg
  to join
- click on op to change
